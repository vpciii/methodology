# ADR NNNN: Short noun-phrase title

- **Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by ADR-XXXX
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Deciders:** names or roles

## Context

What is the situation that forces a decision? Include the
constraints, the forces in tension, and any relevant background. Be
specific. A reader six months from now should be able to reconstruct
why the decision was needed without asking anyone.

## Decision

What did we decide to do? State it in one or two sentences. Use
active voice ("We will…").

## Alternatives considered

For each serious alternative, one short paragraph: what it was, why
it was attractive, why it was rejected.

- **Option A** — …
- **Option B** — …

## Consequences

What becomes easier? What becomes harder? What new constraints does
this decision impose? Include both the upside and the downside
honestly — an ADR that only lists benefits is incomplete.

## References

Links to specs, prior ADRs, external articles, RFCs, or issues that
informed the decision.
