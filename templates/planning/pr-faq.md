## Press release

Write it as if already shipped — one page, plain language a customer
would understand.

- **Headline** — the outcome in one line.
- **Sub-head** — who it's for and the benefit.
- **The problem** — the world before.
- **The solution** — the world after, concretely.
- **Quote** — from you, and from a (hypothetical) user.
- **Getting started** — the smallest first step.
