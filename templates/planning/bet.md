## Appetite

The capacity we are willing to spend — a budget, not an estimate (e.g.
"two weeks, one engineer"). Scope flexes to fit the appetite; the
appetite does not flex to fit the scope.

## Why now

The cost of delay: what does waiting cost, and what makes this the right
bet to place now over the alternatives competing for the same capacity?

## In / out

The slice that fits the appetite, and what is explicitly cut to make it
fit. Feeds the spec's `## Non-goals` and `## Out of scope`.

## Done looks like

The signal that the bet paid off — enough to know whether to renew or
walk away. Sharpens into the spec's success criteria.

## No-go conditions

What would make us *not* place this bet, or stop part-way through.
