# Problem brief: <short name>

- **Status:** Exploring | Validated | Dropped
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Author:** name

> Solution-free on purpose. Describe the problem; do not name a remedy —
> if a solution creeps in, move it to `options.md` (planning.md §1).

## The problem

One or two paragraphs. What is wrong, missing, or worth doing?

## Who feels it

Who notices this today, and how does it show up for them? Be concrete —
a named user, role, or system, not "users" in the abstract.

## Evidence

How do you know this is real? Data, support tickets, observed behavior,
quotes. Distinguish what you have *measured* from what you *suspect*.

## Why now

What makes this worth attention now rather than later — a deadline, a
growing cost, a closing window? If nothing, say so.

## Not the problem

Adjacent problems explicitly out of frame, so the brief stays sharp.
